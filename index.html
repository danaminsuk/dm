TRANSPORTATION

You won't be able to park in the neighborhood on the day of the wedding. We'll arrange for buses to pick you up at one of three locations: the Old Greenwich Metro North Station (New Haven-bound side), the Hyatt Hotel, or the J House Hotel. If you need a place to leave your car during the wedding, the Old Greenwich train station is the best option.

A bus will pick up guests staying at the Hyatt at 3:40pm. Guests staying at the J House will be picked up at 3:50pm. Stops will be made at the Old Greenwich train station at 4:00pm, 4:05pm, and 4:20pm. There will be return shuttle service to the Hyatt, J House, and train station from 8:30pm until 10:30pm

We recommend that those traveling to the wedding by Metro North take the 2:36pm New Haven-bound train, departing from Grand Central, to Old Greenwich.


LODGING

Aside from the Hyatt and J House, you may be able to find lodging at the Marriott in Stamford or the Delamar in Greenwich.
